## Getting Started

### Creating an Account

1. Visit the TechBridge registration page
2. Fill in your details:
   - Full Name
   - Email Address
   - Password (minimum 8 characters with uppercase, lowercase, number, and special character)
   - Date of Birth
3. **For users under 18**: Provide guardian information for parental consent
4. Click "Register" to create your account

### Logging In

1. Enter your email and password
2. Click "Login"
3. **For Admins**: Enter your 6-digit MFA code from your authenticator app

### Changing Language

- Click the language selector (EN/RW) in the top navigation
- Select your preferred language (English or Kinyarwanda)

## For Students

### Enrolling in a Course

1. Navigate to "Courses" from the main menu
2. Browse available courses
3. Click "Enroll" on a course you're interested in
4. Start learning!

### Taking Lessons

1. Go to "Dashboard" to see your enrolled courses
2. Click on a course to view its lessons
3. Click "Start" on a lesson to begin
4. Read the content, watch videos, and download resources
5. Click "Mark as Complete" when finished
6. Earn **10 points** for completing each lesson!

### Taking Quizzes

1. After completing a lesson, scroll down to see available quizzes
2. Click "Take Quiz" to start
3. Answer all questions
4. **Important Rules**:
   - You have **3 attempts** per quiz
   - Must score **60% or higher** to pass
   - Wait **24 hours** between attempts if you fail
5. Earn **20 points** for passing a quiz!

### Submitting Assignments

1. Navigate to the assignment from your course page
2. Read the instructions carefully
3. Upload your file (PDF, DOCX, or other allowed formats)
4. Click "Submit"
5. **Late Submissions**: 10% penalty per day late (maximum 7 days)

### Viewing Your Progress

- **Dashboard**: See overall progress, points, and badges
- **Course Page**: View lesson completion status
- **Profile**: Check your total points and earned badges

1. Go to "Forums" from the main menu

1. Complete all lessons and required assessments in a course
2. Navigate to "Certificates" from the main menu

4. Your certificate includes your name, course name, completion date, and a unique verification code

## For Teachers

### Creating a Course

1. Go to "Dashboard"
2. Click "Create New Course"
3. Fill in course details:
   - Title
   - Description
   - Level (Beginner, Intermediate, Advanced)
   - Category
4. Upload a course image (optional)
5. Click "Create Course"

### Adding Lessons

1. Open your course from the dashboard
2. Click "Add Lesson"
3. Fill in lesson details:
   - Title
   - Order/sequence number
   - Duration
   - Content (supports Markdown formatting)
4. Add resources (PDFs, videos, etc.)
5. Click "Save Lesson"

### Creating Quizzes

1. Navigate to your course
2. Click "Add Quiz"
3. Enter quiz details:
   - Title
   - Time limit (optional)
   - Passing score (default 60%)
   - Select the lesson it's linked to
4. Add questions:
   - Multiple choice
   - True/False
   - Fill in the blank
5. Mark correct answers
6. Click "Publish Quiz"

### Creating Assignments

1. Go to your course page
2. Click "Add Assignment"
3. Set details:
   - Title and instructions
   - Due date
   - Maximum score
   - Allowed file types
4. Click "Create Assignment"

### Grading Submissions

1. Go to "Dashboard" ‚Üí "Assignments"
2. View pending submissions
3. Download and review student work
4. Enter a grade and feedback
5. Click "Submit Grade"

### Monitoring Student Progress

1. Navigate to "Analytics" ‚Üí "Teacher Dashboard"
2. View statistics:
   - Total enrollments
   - Active students
   - Completion rates
   - Average quiz scores
   - Pending grading
3. Click on a specific course to see individual student performance
4. Identify students who need attention (low progress, inactive)

### Communicating with Students

- Reply to forum posts in your courses
- Provide feedback on assignments
- Send announcements through course updates

## For Administrators

### Managing Users

1. Go to "Admin Panel" ‚Üí "Users"
2. View all registered users
3. Actions available:
   - Edit user details
   - Change user roles
   - Deactivate accounts
   - Reset passwords
   - View audit logs

### Setting Up MFA (Two-Factor Authentication)

1. Go to "Profile" ‚Üí "Security"
2. Click "Enable MFA"
3. Scan the QR code with Google Authenticator or similar app
4. Enter the 6-digit code to verify
5. Save backup codes in a secure location
6. MFA is now required for all admin logins

### Viewing System Analytics

1. Navigate to "Admin Panel" ‚Üí "Analytics"
2. View platform-wide statistics:
   - Total users, courses, enrollments
   - Active users
   - Popular courses
   - System performance metrics

### Managing Courses

- Review and approve new courses
- Edit or delete any course
- Monitor course quality and engagement

### System Backups

- **Automatic Backups**:
  - Daily backups at 2:00 AM
  - Monthly backups on the 1st of each month
  - Backups retained for 30 days (daily) or 12 months (monthly)
  
- **Manual Backup** (if needed):
  - Contact system administrator
  - Backups are stored securely and encrypted

### Viewing Audit Logs

1. Go to "Admin Panel" ‚Üí "Audit Logs"
2. Filter by:
   - User
   - Action type
   - Date range
3. Logs include: logins, data changes, security events
4. Logs are retained for 1 year

## FAQ

### How do I reset my password?

1. Click "Forgot Password" on the login page
2. Enter your email address
3. Check your email for a reset link
4. Click the link and create a new password

### Why is my account locked?

Accounts are locked after 5 failed login attempts. Wait 30 minutes or contact support to unlock.

### Can I unenroll from a course?

Yes, but only if you've completed less than 50% of the course. Go to the course page and click "Unenroll".

### How is my final grade calculated?

- 40% from quiz scores
- 40% from assignment grades
- 20% from participation (forum posts, lesson completion)

### What file types can I upload?

- Documents: PDF, DOCX, PPTX, TXT
- Media: MP4, MP3, JPG, PNG
- Archives: ZIP
- Maximum file size: 50MB

### How do I use offline mode?

- Lessons are automatically cached when you view them online
- Access cached lessons even without internet
- Progress syncs automatically when you reconnect (within 30 seconds)

### How long do my sessions last?

- Sessions expire after 30 minutes of inactivity
- You'll need to log in again after expiry

### How can I verify a certificate?

1. Go to the verification page
2. Enter the certificate ID (found on the certificate)
3. View verification details

### Where can I get help?

- Visit the "Forums" to ask questions
- Email support: support@techbridge.com
- Check this user guide for common issues
